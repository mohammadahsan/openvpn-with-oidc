📖 Setup Instructions

1
Download OpenVPN Connect for macOS and install it.
2
Click Download verinvo-vpn.ovpn above.
3
Open OpenVPN Connect → click + → Upload File → select the .ovpn file.
4
Click Connect. A browser window opens — log in with your Oxinus account.
5
After login the browser closes and the VPN tunnel comes up automatically.
